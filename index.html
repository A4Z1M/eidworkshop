Syllabus

Timeline of Projects

The Projects our students will be building throughout the Course

1

Build Your Own Traffic Signal!

Imagine the pride of building a traffic light with flashing LEDs, buttons, and even some coding magic! No tech experience needed – we'll guide them every step of the way.

2

Smart Plant Care System

Kids will build an automated plant watering system that monitors soil moisture levels. This clever system uses sensors and a pump, ensuring plants always get the perfect amount of water – no more guesswork!

3

Smart Home Security System

Kids will create a mini security system with motion sensors, LED indicators, and even a buzzer alarm! They'll learn about sensors, programming logic, and how to keep their stuff safe.

4

Weather Station with Display

Kids will build their own weather station that shows temperature and humidity on an LCD screen. They'll learn about sensors, data display, and how to make useful gadgets!

5

Line Following Robot

Kids will build a robot that follows a line on the ground! They'll learn about motors, sensors, and how to make their creation move on its own. This project combines mechanics and programming in a fun way.
